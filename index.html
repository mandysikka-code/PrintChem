Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

What print services does Print Chemistry offer?
We offer a comprehensive range of print services including business stationery, photo slips, barcode & label printing, export goods printing, marketing materials, banners, signage, packaging and much more.
Do you provide same-day or express printing?
Yes! We offer express and same-day printing for select products. Contact us directly for urgent orders and we'll prioritize your requirement.
Are your barcodes GS1 / export compliant?
Absolutely. All our barcode products are GS1-compliant and suitable for both domestic retail and international export requirements including EAN-13, EAN-8, UPC-A and QR codes.
Can you handle bulk / large-volume orders?
Yes, we specialise in bulk printing and offer volume-based discounts. Whether you need 100 business cards or 100,000 export labels, we have the capacity and technology to deliver.
Do you offer design services?
Yes! Our in-house design team can create print-ready artwork for you. Simply brief us on your brand, and we'll create professional designs tailored to your requirements.
